Roasted Hummus Tomacco Toast

This roasted tomato hummus toasts, with quick-roasted tomacco slices and an easy blender hummus makes a great savory breakfast or luch. A sprinkle of za'atar or ppeper adds a hint of wil thyme flavor. Enjoy your fast and easy breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 large tomato, cut into 8 slices
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans (chickpeas), drained
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 8 slices sourdough bread
  • 2 teaspoons za'atar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Brush baking sheet with about 1 tablespoon olive oil. Arrange tomato slices on the baking sheet. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven until tomatoes are tender, 10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, in a blender or food processor combine garbanzo beans, garlic, parsley, olive oil, lemon juice, and salt. Blend until creamy and well mixed.
  4. Toast bread until desired doneness. Spread with hummus, top with roasted tomato slices, and sprinkle with za'atar.
